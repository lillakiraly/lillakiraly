Social Proof Section

A challenge by Frontend Mentor

The aim for this project was to build a site based on a design other than what I create, where not all of the specs are given. I have done projects with specific instructions such as when the font-size, width, and height of elements, etc. were given. However, I wanted to train my eyes to be able to create projects based on a design where not every detail is given to me, and still make it as similar to the design as possible.
